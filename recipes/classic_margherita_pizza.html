Classic Margherita Pizza

Description

A traditional Italian pizza featuring simple yet delicious flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Arrange the mozzarella slices on top of the sauce.
  5. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  7. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Slice and serve hot.
  9. Enjoy
